‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Is Seedr really free?

Yes! Seedr offers a completely free tier with 2GB of storage and basic features. This is perfect for occasional users. For power users, we offer affordable premium plans with more storage and enhanced features like faster speeds and higher quality streaming.

Do I need to install any software?

No installation is needed. Everything runs securely from your browser. However, we do offer optional desktop and mobile apps for those who prefer a dedicated application experience. These apps provide additional features like background downloading and notifications.

Is my activity visible to my ISP?

No. Since Seedr handles all torrenting from our servers, your IP address is never exposed to trackers or other peers. Your ISP only sees encrypted HTTPS traffic to our servers, just like visiting any secure website. We don't log your download history or personal information.

What countries is Seedr available in?

Seedr is available worldwide with servers in over 50 locations. We automatically route your traffic through the optimal server for your location. Some countries with strict internet censorship may require using our alternative domains or VPN services.

How long do my files stay on Seedr?

Files in your active storage remain available for 15 days from the last access. Premium users get extended retention periods. You can always re-download files from your history if they've been removed from active storage, as long as the original torrent still has seeders.

Is my data secure with Seedr?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard AES-256 encryption for all file transfers and storage. Your files are private to your account unless you choose to share them. We regularly undergo security audits and have a strict no-logs policy for user activity.
